My Story

I was on the city bus from a doctor's apt for a work accident when the city bus wrecked. It could have been worse, but I had whiplash throughout my spine and I was unable to run for four months. I had a back brace and physical therapy with chiropractic four times a week.

I was in pain and I turned to food as a comfort instead of as a fuel ( If you get stuck in that mental space then I will bring that formula later in my blog). As you can see there, I had a big stack of pancakes. I ate like a runner, even when I wasn't a runner and it caught up to me.

It was hard to get out of that mental funk, when there was so much I felt like I couldn't do and that first mile made all the difference. Then in January that following year they thought that I had a heart issue because my EKG 'as abnormal. I was at an all time low. I was not able to do ANY cardio activity and I was nervous as hell that something was wrong with my heart and turns out after seeing a cardiologist that they gave me a clean bill of health

Then I thought to myself "Whats my excuse?"

So I trained hard and completed my first half marathon in spring of 2016. I did a second half in the fall that year. I was at the finisher party for the IronMan Louisville and then was when I thought " This is my next big goal. This is what I was meant to do". That was the start of my journey. Thank you for listening and I hope you will be here along with me as I continue my journey.
